NSBE Vision

We envision a world in which engineering is a mainstream word in homes and communities of color
, and all Black students can envision themselves as engineers. In this world,
Blacks exceed parity in entering engineering fields, earning degrees, and succeeding professionally.

NSBE Mission

The mission of the National Society of Black Engineers is
""to increase the number of culturally responsible Black Engineers
who excel academically, succeed professionally and positively impact the community.""
